About Unkit

I used to help create some of the most-loved salad kits in the country.

Now I’m building the version I actually want at home.

The problem

You buy great greens. And then... nothing pulls it together.

Maybe from the farmer’s market, maybe the $6 plastic box, maybe from your garden. The base is great.

But everything else is an afterthought — weak dressing, no crunch, nothing pulling it together.

Soggy, wilted salad kits aren’t cutting it either.

01

Texture

The part most salads are missing: crisp, crunchy, satisfying contrast.

02

Balance

03

Ease

No prep project. No sad desk lunch. Just add your base and protein.

Why I built this

I wanted the magic of a great salad kit — without the sad greens.

I worked on the kinds of foods people grab when they don’t have time to think — including salad kits you’ve probably seen before.

One of my go-to recipes — the kind I bring to dinner parties and always get asked about — became a top-selling kit.

And still, at home, I didn’t want the kit itself. I wanted the good parts: the dressing, the crunch, the flavor, the thing that makes lunch feel done.

I’m also a busy mom. Lunch is usually whatever I can throw together between everything else. I built Unkit to make what I already had actually worth eating.
