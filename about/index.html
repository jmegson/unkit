The problem

You buy great greens. And then... nothing pulls it together.

Maybe from the farmer’s market, maybe the $6 plastic box, maybe from your garden. The base is great. But everything else is an afterthought — weak dressing, no crunch, nothing pulling it together. Soggy, wilted salad kits aren’t cutting it either.

Why I built this

I wanted the magic of a great salad kit — without the sad greens.

I worked on the kinds of foods people grab when they don’t have time to think — including salad kits you’ve probably seen before. One of my go-to recipes, the kind I bring to dinner parties and always get asked about, became a top-selling kit. But at home, I didn’t want the kit itself. I wanted the good parts: the dressing, the crunch, the flavor, the thing that makes lunch feel done. I’m also a busy mom, and I built Unkit to make what I already had actually worth eating.
