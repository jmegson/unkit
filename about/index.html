About Unkit

I used to help create some of the most-loved salad kits in the country. Now I'm building the version I actually want at home.

Unkit makes flavor-packed add-on kits for the greens, grains, and market hauls you already have. Real dressing, real crunch, real balance — done in under 2 minutes.

Why I built this

Built by someone who has spent a lot of time thinking about salad kits.

I used to work on the kinds of foods people grab when they don’t have time to think — including salad kits you’ve probably seen before.

I learned what actually makes something craveable: not just dressing, but texture, balance, contrast.

One of my go-to salad recipes — the kind I bring to dinner parties and always get asked about — ended up becoming a top-selling kit.

And still, at home, I didn’t want the kit itself. I wanted something better.

I’m also a busy mom. Lunch is usually whatever I can throw together between everything else. I didn’t want another “solution.” I wanted something that made what I already had actually worth eating.

Our belief

Food doesn’t need to be complicated to be good.

But it does need to be intentional. A good dressing matters. Crunch matters. Balance matters. Small upgrades can completely change the outcome.
