About Unkit

You already have the base. We make it craveable.

Unkit makes flavor-packed add-on kits for the greens, grains, and market hauls you already have. Real dressing, real crunch, real balance — done in under 2 minutes.

The problem

Most salads don’t fail because of the greens.

They fail because everything else is an afterthought.

The dressing is weak. There’s no crunch. Nothing pulls it together. So even good ingredients can still end up feeling like a compromise.

Why I built this

Built by someone who has spent a lot of time thinking about salad kits.

I’ve spent years working on ready-to-eat food — the kind where you test what people actually crave, not just what looks healthy.

Over time, one pattern kept showing up: the base was usually fine. The rest is what made or broke it.

I’m also a busy mom. I don’t have time for elaborate lunches, but I also don’t want something that feels like settling.

Unkit is the middle: simple upgrades, real ingredients, and a lunch you actually want.

Our belief

Food doesn’t need to be complicated to be good.

But it does need to be intentional. A good dressing matters. Crunch matters. Balance matters. Small upgrades can completely change the outcome.
